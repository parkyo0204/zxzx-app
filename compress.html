Why Compress Images?

Large image files slow down your website, consume bandwidth, and hurt SEO rankings. Google uses page speed as a ranking factor, and images often account for 50-80% of a page's total weight.

By compressing your images, you can:

  • Reduce page load time by 2-5 seconds
  • Improve Google Core Web Vitals scores
  • Save storage space on your device or cloud
  • Reduce bandwidth costs for your hosting
  • Improve user experience on mobile devices

Frequently Asked Questions

Is image compression really free?

Yes, ZXZX is completely free with no hidden costs. There are no file size limits, no watermarks, and no registration required. You can compress unlimited images.

Will compression reduce image quality?

At medium settings (80%), compression is virtually invisible to the human eye. Even at aggressive settings (40%), most images retain acceptable quality for web use. You can compare before and after using our preview tool.

What's the difference between JPG, PNG, and WebP compression?

JPG is best for photographs. PNG is best for graphics with transparency. WebP offers the best compression for both types. Converting to WebP typically saves 25-35% compared to JPG.

Are my images uploaded to your server?

No. All compression happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device. This is why ZXZX is one of the safest image tools available.

Can I compress images on my phone?

Yes. ZXZX works on all modern mobile browsers including Chrome and Safari on iOS and Android. The interface is fully responsive.
