Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to your server?

No. All image processing happens directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device. This makes ZXZX one of the most private image tools available.

What image formats are supported?

ZXZX supports JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and SVG for various operations. Convert between any supported format.

Is there a file size limit?

You can process files up to 20MB each, with up to 30 files per batch. For most use cases, this is more than sufficient.

Does compression reduce image quality?

Our smart compression algorithm reduces file size by up to 80% while maintaining visually identical quality. You can compare before/after with our preview tool.

Can I use ZXZX on my phone?

Yes. ZXZX is fully responsive and works on all modern mobile browsers including Chrome, Safari, and Firefox on iOS and Android.
