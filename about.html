About ZXZX

We build free, privacy-first image tools that run entirely in your browser. No uploads, no servers, no data collection.

Our Story

ZXZX was born from a simple observation: most online image tools require you to upload your files to remote servers. This creates unnecessary privacy risks, slows down the workflow, and often comes with hidden costs like watermarks, file limits, or mandatory registration.

We believed there was a better way. Modern web browsers are incredibly powerful — they can process images, run complex algorithms, and handle batch operations entirely on your device. So we built ZXZX: a platform that leverages browser-native technologies like the Canvas API, WebAssembly, and modern JavaScript to deliver professional-grade image processing without ever touching your files.

Since launching in 2024, ZXZX has helped millions of users compress, convert, resize, and optimize their images. We've become a trusted tool for web developers, photographers, designers, content creators, and everyday users who need quick, reliable image processing without compromising their privacy.

Our Mission

ZXZX exists to make powerful image processing accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free. We believe that basic image operations like compression, format conversion, and resizing should not require expensive software, technical expertise, or surrendering your data to third parties.

Our core principles guide everything we build:

  • Privacy First — Every operation runs locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted. When you close the tab, your data disappears completely.
  • Truly Free — No hidden costs, no watermarks, no file size traps, no "free trial" tricks. Every feature is available to every user, with no registration required.
  • Quality Driven — We use the same compression and processing algorithms found in professional tools. You get professional results without the professional price tag.
  • Universal Access — ZXZX works on any device with a modern browser — desktop, tablet, or phone. No downloads, no installations, no app store required.

How ZXZX Works (Technical Overview)

ZXZX uses the HTML5 Canvas API for image rendering and manipulation. When you upload an image, it's loaded directly into browser memory using the FileReader API. All processing — compression, format conversion, resizing, and metadata stripping — happens through client-side JavaScript and WebAssembly modules.

This architecture means there is no server processing, no data transfer, and no storage on our end. The only network requests ZXZX makes are for loading the page itself and serving advertisements to keep the service free. Your actual image data never leaves your browser's memory.

We continuously optimize our algorithms to deliver the best compression ratios, fastest processing speeds, and highest output quality. Our team monitors advancements in browser APIs and WebAssembly capabilities to ensure ZXZX stays at the cutting edge of browser-based image processing.
